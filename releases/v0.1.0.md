## RaidPunishUI v0.1.0

### Added
- Click moderation flow from player nickname in chat.
- Action selection for mute, warn, ban, and ipban.
- Duration entry with day/hour format.
- Reason selection and custom reason input.
- Built-in presets based on RaidMine rules 2.1-3.10.

### Notes
- Client-side moderation helper.
- Requires server commands `/mute`, `/warn`, `/ban`, `/ipban` to exist.
